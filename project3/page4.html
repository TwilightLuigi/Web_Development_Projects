A Very Bref History Of Soccer

Soccer got it's start over 2,000 years ago in ancient China. However it was the British that formed the game into what we know today. They are credited with recording the first uniform rules for the sport and over the years more and more rules have been added. Some examples being the penatly kick in 1891 and the use of red and yellow card rules in 1970. Some other history to note is that FIFA became a memeber of the International Football Association in 1913.
